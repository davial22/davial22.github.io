<h2>Rules in basketball</h2>


<p>The game is played on a rectangular court with two teams of five players.The court is divided by a mid-court line. The offensive team has 10 seconds to get the ball over the mid-court line. Basketball rules include how to score, how to move the ball, and how to avoid fouls.  </p>
